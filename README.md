## About Me

I'm a backend-focused developer with a multidisciplinary background in healthcare and web application development.

Before moving into software, I completed vocational studies in **Pharmacy and Parapharmacy** and **Nutrition and Dietetics**. I later completed a Higher Vocational Training program in **Web Application Development (DAW)**, turning my interest in technology into a new professional direction.

Today, I'm focused on building reliable and maintainable backend systems with **Python**, while developing a deeper understanding of APIs, relational databases, testing, architecture, and software engineering fundamentals. I'm also currently specializing in **Artificial Intelligence and Big Data**.

My healthcare background gives me valuable domain knowledge and a different perspective when approaching technical problems, without limiting me to a single industry. My goal is to combine that experience with backend engineering, data, and AI to contribute to useful, responsible software products.
